Grubhub Corporate Group Orders

Grubhub is an online food-ordering and delivery marketplace. We built functionality that connected restaurants with businesses who need to feed their employees. Employees place individual orders online, then restaurants fulfill those orders, to be delivered together at a specific, scheduled time. This gave restaurants repeat clients, larger orders, and exposure to new customers, and consolidated corporate revenue under one platform for Grubhub.
