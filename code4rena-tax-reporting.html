Code4rena

Contractor Tax Reporting

Code4rena is a security platform that leverages a global network of independent contractors. To ensure OFAC compliance, we would verify their identities and collect their tax reporting information before paying them, using a third-party service to do so.

As a startup, we needed to be as capital efficient as possible. We were paying exorbitant fees for compliance services, often for contractors we wouldn’t ever need to pay. We quickly built an in-house solution that balanced our security and compliance needs, and made a meaningful impact on our burn rate.
