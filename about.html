👋 Nice to meet you.

I’m a product designer based out of Raleigh, North Carolina. I'm working on something new.

Through design thinking, I help organizations transform a business strategy into a human experience in digital products. Since 2013, I’ve worked with people from startup entrepreneurs building their first MVP to large organizations iterating on enterprise products serving millions of people.

I believe design is a team sport, and the best teams are built with intent. I help design teams mature, developing junior and mid-level designers, and improving processes to make teams more effective.

I value…

  • Ethics: Creating value while building a just world. The business of doing good is good for business.
  • Candor: Communicating with truth, tempered with kindness. Empowerment through vulnerability.
  • Purpose: Moving teams towards a mission-driven future, and executing to get there incrementally.
  • Ownership: Being an owner, not an agent. Accountability over ego.
  • Meritocracy: Decision-making using the best ideas, regardless of where they come from.
  • Growth: Always a student. Never stop learning.
